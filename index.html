About Me

Hi there! I'm Justin. I am an Operations Manager for Amazon in Denver, CO. About three months into the COVID-19 Pandemic I decided that I was going to learn how to code. I signed up for Codeacademy and watched hours of Khan Academy and YouTube videos on HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. When I felt confident I signed up for a Full Stack Coding Bootcamp through the University of Denver. I'm six months into this new endeavor and I'm absolutely in love with what I am learning. I'm unsure how I'm going to be using the skills I gain in this course, but I'm excited to learn them with everyone.

I was born in Honolulu, Hawaii and lived there for only three years. I graduated from High School in Pueblo West Colorado and attended college at the University of Colorado - Denver where I majored in Biology.

In my spare time I like to play video games, snowboard, watch new movies, and spend hours on Reddit. I am also really loving learning how to code! I plan on integrating an online journal into this portfolio to chronicle my adventure into the world of coding. Stay tuned for many exciting modifications to the website.
